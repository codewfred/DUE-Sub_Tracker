Frequently
Asked Questions

How does the app track my subscriptions automatically?

Users usually wonder if they need to add everything manually or if the app scans emails, bank statements, etc. We use secure bank integration to automatically detect recurring payments.

Will the app notify me before a subscription renews?

Yes! You can set custom alerts for each subscription to get notified days before a renewal payment is due.

Is my financial and personal data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and never store your banking credentials. Your data is yours alone.

Can I cancel subscriptions through the app?

For supported services, we offer a one-tap cancellation feature. For others, we provide direct links and step-by-step guides.
